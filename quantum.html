Quantum Mechanics Research Paper

April 2017

I took Quantum Mechanics III (8.06) during in Spring 2017. As part of the course, students write a research paper in the style of a journal article on a known topic related to quantum mechanics. I wrote my paper on vibration-rotation spectrscopy of diatomic molecules, leading the reader through understandging how to interpret the vibration-rotation spectrum of HCl. The paper, which received one of the highest grades in the class, can be viewed here.
